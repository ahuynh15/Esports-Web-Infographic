Background

You might be saying to yourself right now, "What is an E-SPORT? What about LIVE STREAMING?"

Bascially, e-sports are competitions between individuals or teams from all around the world that take place inside the digital world of videogames.

On the other hand, live streaming, in a sense, is media (music, shows, gameplay, etc.) broadcasted live over the internet.
